Arab International Ltd. is a leading manpower recruiting company based in Bangladesh. We specialize in sending labors, students and visitors to foreign countries like Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Dubai, Sudan, Europe and Canada for work, study and tour. Our mission is to help people find jobs and to help companies find the right employees. We believe that everyone deserves a chance to succeed and we are committed to making that happen.
